Selected Works

Urtu Founder & CEO

View Process & Gallery

Urtu is Mongolia's first mobile C2C marketplace and on-demand delivery service, incubated through Lever, Inc. I led a team of 6 (including 4 engineers) and drove development to completion on 4 beta products for iOS and web in three months. I also led the design & business side and contributed significantly to frontend and iOS development. Urtu is currently in a closed-beta phase and will launch in late Fall. Product Involvement: UI/UX Design, Front-End Engineering (HTML, CSS, JS), Product Management, iOS Development (Swift, Xcode), Some back-end development (Ruby on Rails), Business Planning & Strategy

NiuPiao Founder & CEO

View Process & Gallery

NiuPiao was originally a ticketing concept for Asia, but later pivoted to become Urtu. Some of Mongolia's top popstars and entertainers (Bold and X-tuts, to be exact) agreed to sell all of their tickets (worth $11mn/year!) through us. We also won Grand Prize of $15,000 at the Williams Business Plan Challenge and were finalists at the Draper Business Plan Competition, where I pitched to Tim Draper and other investors. Product Involvement: UI/UX Design, Product Management, Business Planning & Strategy

85 Main St. Design Thinker
